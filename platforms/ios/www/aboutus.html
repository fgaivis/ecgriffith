About Us

     Griffith Real Estate Services markets and manages real estate assets owned by the E.C. Griffith Company. Griffith Real Estate Services was formed in 1992 but its real estate roots and history can be traced back to E.C. Griffith, Sr. and The E.C. Griffith Company, which began operation in 1912. As one of Charlotte‘s oldest family owned real estate firms, it has developed some of Charlotte‘s leading institutions for the past 102 years.
